COVID-19 is a highly contagious disease caused by the SARS-CoV-2 virus, which primarily spreads through the air via infectious droplets and aerosol particles.

COVID-19 most often causes respiratory symptoms that can feel much like a cold, the flu, or pneumonia. COVID-19 may affect more than your lungs and respiratory system. Other parts of your body may also be affected by the disease. Most people with COVID-19 have mild symptoms, but some people become severely ill.

COVID-19 changed the world in many ways, affecting health systems, education, businesses, and daily life across the globe. During the pandemic, technology and programming played an important role in helping people stay connected and informed. Developers created websites, mobile apps, and data dashboards to track cases, share medical updates, and support online learning and remote work. Artificial intelligence and data analysis were also used to study the spread of the virus and assist researchers in developing treatments and vaccines.
